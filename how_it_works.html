How DataCroc works?

DataCroc Platform is designed with strong focus on user delight and happiness. Getting started is easy, and we'll help you in every step.

Developers, Product Managers, Operations Managers, Everyone will fall in love with it.

Tutorials and Documentation coming soon.

1. Feed the Events to the DataCroc Platform.

Our easy to use SDKs make it super simple to push events from anywhere in your product stack into the DataCroc platform.

Simple and easy to integrate SDKs to help push the events.
Let the DataCroc do the heavy lifting.
Save engineers from maintaining rules, alerts and integrations
Increase developer productivity and happiness.

2. Define Patterns and Filters that matter to you.

With our easy to use interface anyone can define patterns to be matched against the stream of events.

Define event patterns using a simple interface.
DataCroc continuously monitors the stream of events to match against these patterns.
Create filters for event routing.
Relax, DataCroc has got your back.

Identify and Respond to meaningful event patterns as they happen

Never let an opportunity, anomaly or fraud go unnoticed.

Our Pattern Detection Engine, continuously monitors your stream of business events against pre-defined rules to surface meaningful composite events.

3. Connect Patterns and Filters to Integrations.

Use our suite of ready made integrations with 100s of apps that you already love or create your custom integrations.

Connect to apps and automate taking actions.
Talk to your apps the event-driven way.
Let DataCroc manage talking to your applications for you.
Free yourself from adding and maintaining integrations.
